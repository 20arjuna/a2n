Audio2Notes. Content in outline format instantly.

The Problem

We live in a world full of content. Yet, even in the 21st century, it is difficult to get to the meaning and crux of information.

That's why we created A2N.

A2N is revolutionizing the way we process content by synthesizing audio based information into a simple, easy to read outline.
Gone are the days where we waste hours of time listening to audio or video, only to realize a small portion of it was relevant or important.

Welcome to A2N.

Use Cases

Enterprise

Tired of having to listen to hours of meeting discussions? Use A2N to quickly summarize your meetings so you can get all the crucial information without the headache of listening to the conversation.

Persons with Hearing Disabilities

For those who are unfortunately unable to listen to lectures, presentations, or meetings, A2N is for you! All content will be synthesized from an audio format to outlined formatted notes so that you too can grasp the information from these mediums.

School

At A2N, we know firsthand the struggles of taking notes and engaging in class at the same time. That’s why A2N is the perfect tool for you; leave A2N to take notes of the teacher’s speech while you focus on listening and participating in class conversation.

How It Works

Upload File

Upload your file to our application directly from your computer or the cloud. We now support audio of any format and length, including .wav, .mp4, .mp3, and .m4a.

Machine Learning, AI

Using advanced machine learning techniques, our algorithm is able to quickly and efficiently produce concise, outlined notes from audio. A neural network architecture is trained with thousands of audio files to ensure maximum performance.
